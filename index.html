Welcome to Hack CFA

This is a brand new competition at Carnegie Mellon sponsored by the Mellon College of Science. Students will be able to submit their design proposals for a new sculpture on campus that says something about the world of science. The winning group will then receive $3500 to fund their project! The sculpture will be displayed on campus for one year (until the next Hack CFA) and then be donated to a nearby school.

Rules and Requirements:

    Teams are made up of 4-8 students, but at least one has to be from the College of Fine Arts and at least one from the Mellon College of Science.
    The proposed sculpture must be no larger than six feet by six feet by six feet, must be able to stand on its own, and permenantly withstand the outdoors.
    Each team must submit a paragraph prosopal, a list of materials, a timeline of expected deadlines, and the estimated cost.
    You will have to create an object in Maya or Blender, and then export it to Fusion 360 by Autodesk run in order to test your designs.
    The judges will then determine whether or not your prosopal is feasable and able to withstand outdoor condiitons. Then they will decide a winner.
    The winning team will have four months to build their project and once finished, it will be displayed for everybody on campus.
